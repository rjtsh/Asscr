Welcome to Asscr!

Our Goal: here at Asscr , our main goal is to provide help to the vulnerable populations from Global pandemics and disease outbreaks.
we aim to reduce the spread of the diseases by providing convenient and simple information with ease of access
to reliable information to anyone and everyone.

How we operate: We give you accurate information on the most common diseases and their major and minor symptoms
the information provided should help you get an understanding on what possible diseases you could have and if a checkup is neccessary.
if you are feeling other symptons that are not related to these major diseases , our chatbot interaction can provide you with an
good idea on what are the most likely possibilities based on your location.
